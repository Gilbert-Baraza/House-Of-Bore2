Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding shipping, returns, and bespoke orders.

What is your return and restoration policy? ↓

We accept complimentary worldwide returns within 30 days of delivery for all unworn garments in their original packaging. Furthermore, all House of Bore leather goods carry a lifetime restoration guarantee.

How long does international express delivery take? ↓

Orders dispatched to North America, Europe, and East Asia arrive within 2 to 3 business days via carbon-neutral express courier. Signature is required upon delivery.

Do you offer bespoke tailoring or made-to-order sizing? ↓

Yes. Discerning clients visiting our London Savile Row or Paris Saint-Honoré salons can book private consultations for made-to-measure outerwear and suiting.

How do I care for full-grain leather goods and cashmere knitwear? ↓

We recommend storing knitwear folded in breathable cotton garment bags and applying our natural beeswax conditioner to leather artifacts twice yearly. Detailed care guides accompany every order.

Can I schedule a private styling appointment at your showrooms? ↓

Yes. Please contact our concierge team at concierge@houseofbore.com at least 48 hours in advance to arrange a private viewing and fitting at our London or Paris locations.
