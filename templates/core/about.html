The Philosophy of Enduring Craft

In an era dominated by hyper-accelerated production and synthetic obsolescence, House of Bore operates as an antidote. We partner exclusively with multi-generational family workshops across Biella, Florence, and the Scottish Highlands—artisans who have spent decades perfecting single weaves and vegetable tanning formulas.

Our design process begins not with mood boards or seasonal palettes, but with raw fiber analysis. We test tensile strength, abrasion resistance, and aging characteristics before drafting a single pattern. If an artifact cannot survive ten years of daily wear and graceful aging, it does not enter our collection.
